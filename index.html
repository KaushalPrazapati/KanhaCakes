Our Story

A Legacy of Taste & Purity

Located in the heart of Katihar at Kali Bari Road, Kanha Cakes N Snacks is your premium destination for pure vegetarian delights. We believe that every celebration deserves a centerpiece crafted with passion, quality ingredients, and utmost hygiene.

From handcrafted custom cakes for weddings and birthdays to savory snacks and shakes, we provide a family-friendly atmosphere where every bite tells a story.
