Our Sweet Story

Welcome to GOLD BAKERS, Patna's premier destination for luxury cakes and bakes. Nestled in the heart of Ashiyana Digha main road, we believe that every celebration deserves a touch of gold.

Our expert bakers use only the finest, freshest ingredients to craft visually stunning and deliciously addictive cakes, pastries, and artisanal breads. From bespoke wedding cakes to quick midnight cravings, we are here to bake memories for you, open till 1 AM.
